About me

FULBRIGHT scholar originally from Colombia. First year MS student in Biostatistics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Graduated from the National University of Colombia in 2020 with a major in Statistics.

Graduate Research Assistant for the Hispanic Community Health Study / Study of Latinos (HCHS/SOL) at the Collaborative Studies Coordinading Center (CSCC). Responsible for assisting DrPH. Daniela Sotres-Alvarez in the ancilliary study Family Lifestyle Outcomes Research (SOL FLOR) and Stress Gender and Minority - Study of Latinos (SGM SOL).

I love coding. So I am always working on some project or learning something new about SAS, R or SQL, and sometimes Python, Julia, Javascript, HTML or CSS. I came to web development tools when I became interested in data visualization (d3.js) and to create my own website. Proficient in R, SAS and SQL.

Research interests

Bayesian statistics and programming, survey sampling, spatial demography and biostatistics.
